ERIC SWALWELL TO PARTICIPATE IN TWO EVENTS FRIDAY IN SAN FRANCISCO

DUBLIN, CA – 2020 Democratic presidential candidate Congressman Eric Swalwell will participate in two events Friday, June 14 in San Francisco.

  • 10:45amPT – “Road to the White House” Town Hall Series with Silicon Valley Leadership Group & KQED Radio. At the offices of Scoop, 22 4th St. (9th Floor), San Francisco, CA 94103. OPEN TO PRESS BUT NOT TO GENERAL PUBLIC.

     
  • 6:15pmPT – Rep. Eric Swalwell: Presidential Candidate at the Commonwealth Club, 110 The Embarcadero (Taube Family Auditorium), San Francisco, CA 94105. OPEN TO PRESS; TICKETS REQUIRED FOR GENERAL PUBLIC, AVAILABLE AT https://www.commonwealthclub.org/events/2019-06-14/rep-eric-swalwell-presidential-candidate

MEDIA ATTENDING EITHER OF THESE EVENTS MUST PLEASE RSVP TO PRESS@ERICSWALWELL.COM.

Swalwell, 38, announced his candidacy for President of the United States in April. With a rallying cry of “Go big, be bold, do good,” Swalwell is putting his experience and energy to work for America. He was first in his family to go to college and served as a prosecutor and city councilman before winning his House seat in 2012. His campaign priorities include ending gun violence; providing healthcare for all and finding cures for our deadliest diseases; reducing student loan debt; and combating climate chaos. He’s fighting to bring the promise of America – that hard work must lead to a better life for you and your family – to all Americans.

Learn more about the candidate’s background and plan at EricSwalwell.com and follow him on Twitter at @EricSwalwell.
