Jay's Freedom from Fossil Fuels Plan transforms our economy by taking on Big Oil and Big Coal in five ways:

  1. Ending Fossil Fuel Subsidies: We'll eliminate all federal subsidies for oil, gas, and coal companies, worth over $20 billion each year, by repealing tax credits and deductions.
  2. Banning New Federal Leases and Phasing Out Fossil Fuel Production Nationwide: We need to take immediate executive action to ban new fossil fuel leasing on public lands and offshore waters, restrict drilling and mining under existing leases, and permanently ban fracking -- for good.
  3. Holding Polluters Accountable: Jay will force corporations to pay for the costs of their pollution and reestablish meaningful enforcement of federal environmental laws -- instead of Donald Trump's history of letting Big Oil and Big Coal off scot-free.
  4. No New Fossil Fuel Infrastructure: We'll require all federal agencies to reject fossil fuel infrastructure, and empower states, tribal nations, and local governments in infrastructure decision-making. We'll end fossil fuel infrastructure right here and now.
  5. Improving Corporate Climate Accountability: We'll appoint members of the Securities & Exchange Commission and the Commodity Futures Trading Commission who will pursue measures for corporate disclosure of climate change risks and commitments and fossil fuel holdings.
