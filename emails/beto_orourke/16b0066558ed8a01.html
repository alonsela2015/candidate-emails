Today, HBO premieres a brand new documentary, Running with Beto, which tells the story of Beto’s Senate campaign and thousands of volunteers and supporters who stepped up to change Texas politics forever. It shows an important glimpse into what’s possible if each of us steps up to change politics in this country, so we’re inviting you to watch it.

This campaign was different. Beto traveled to all 254 counties in Texas to talk to and listen to people -- no one overlooked or left out. As a result, Beto shattered turnout records and earned more votes than any previous statewide Democratic campaign. And it laid the groundwork for a historic presidential campaign that's going to every corner of this country to reconnect Americans with our democracy.

Want to watch Running with Beto? You can see it tonight on HBO at 8pm ET or anytime after on HBO Go and HBO Now.

We know not everyone has HBO, and we want to make sure as many people as possible can see for themselves what was possible in Texas and what is possible in every community across the country.
